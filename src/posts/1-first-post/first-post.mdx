# Welcome to Lisbon!

Lisbon is the sunny and cosmopolitan capital of Portugal. The city breathes music, culture, art and food, and tech. 
Lisbon has a growing tech scene, is home to many tech copmanies and hosts several popular tech conferences and festivals.
